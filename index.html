What is the difference between a muffin and a cupcake?

The mixing process and the consistency of the batter differentiates cupcakes and muffins.
Cupcakes are made by creaming the butter and sugar together to create a smooth, fluffy batter.
... Muffin batter, on the other hand, is beaten briefly and remains relatively lumpy.
This makes for a more dense baked good.
